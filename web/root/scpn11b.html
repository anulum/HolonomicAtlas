Geometry

Information Network Topology

The noosphere's structure is modeled as a complex network (e.g., scale-free, small-world). The geometry of this network—its hubs, clusters, and path lengths—determines the dynamics of memetic propagation and the potential for global phase coherence.

Phase Space of Ideas

The collective set of all ideas and beliefs can be represented as a high-dimensional "phase space." Cultural evolution is a trajectory through this space, guided by attractors representing dominant paradigms and mytho-symbolic codes.

Mathematics

Memetic Field Equations

The spread of memes is modeled using reaction-diffusion equations. The "meme density" field evolves based on propagation through communication networks, decay (forgetting), and sourcing from individual minds.

Phase Transition Models

The emergence of a coherent noosphere is modeled as a phase transition. When network connectivity or coupling strength crosses a critical threshold, the system spontaneously shifts from a disordered state to a globally synchronized state, described by a mean-field order parameter.

Physics

Information Entropy & Coherence

The noosphere is a thermodynamic system governed by information entropy. Coherent states (shared paradigms) are low-entropy, ordered configurations. The system evolves by balancing entropy (novelty, diversity) with coherence (shared understanding, stability).

Ψ–Noosphere Coupling

A physical coupling is proposed between the individual consciousness field ($\Psi_s$) and the noospheric field. This interaction is energetically favorable when the individual's mental state is in phase with the collective, creating a feedback loop that reinforces cultural norms and shared realities.
