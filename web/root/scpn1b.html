Quantum State Vector

$$|\psi_{MT}\rangle = \sum_{i} c_i(t) |s_1, s_2, ..., s_N\rangle_i$$

$$\hat{H}_{MT} = \sum_{i} \epsilon_i \hat{\sigma}_{z}^{(i)} + \sum_{i \neq j} J_{ij} (\hat{\sigma}_{+}^{(i)}\hat{\sigma}_{-}^{(j)} + \text{h.c.})$$

Physics

Quantum Zeno Effect

The primary mechanism for protecting quantum states. The manuscript proposes that the Ψ-field's rapid, repeated coupling to a coherent state prevents environmental decoherence, extending the state's lifetime to biologically relevant timescales.

Frohlich Condensation

The physical process by which microtubules achieve a macroscopic quantum state. Metabolic energy pumps the tubulin lattice into a Bose-Einstein-like condensate, causing it to oscillate coherently at GHz-THz frequencies.

Quantum Tunneling

A key process in neural gates and enzyme function. The Ψ-field can subtly modulate the potential barriers, influencing tunneling probabilities and thereby altering the rates of critical biochemical reactions like neurotransmitter release.
