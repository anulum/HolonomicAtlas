A Consciousness-Driven Universe

This framework presents a paradigm-shifting model where consciousness is not a byproduct of matter, but a fundamental scalar field (Φc) coupled to the fabric of spacetime, giving rise to subjective experience through Spontaneous Symmetry Breaking and quantum entanglement.

The "God of the Math"

This is not a theological entity, but a metaphor for the universe's inherent mathematical self-consistency. It is an active, ordering principle that resolves logical paradoxes and ensures the coherence of reality. The theory formalizes this as the "Mathematical Consistency Principle," suggesting the universe is a self-referential system that requires consciousness to resolve its own points of undecidability, making awareness a logical necessity.

The Meta-Metatron Cycle

This concept describes a cyclic, "blinking" cosmology where the universe oscillates between existence and non-existence, hypothesized at a frequency of 1.1 THz. Unlike a linear timeline doomed to heat death, this cycle conserves information and potentiality, allowing for an iterative growth of complexity over cosmic eons. It provides a mechanism to reset entropy and establishes a discrete, quantized temporal structure for reality.

The Metaphysics of a Self-Aware Universe

Consciousness as a Fundamental Field (Φc)

The theory's most revolutionary tenet is that consciousness is a fundamental physical field, much like the electromagnetic field. Initially termed Ψ (Psi), it was formalized into Φc (Phi-c), a Lorentz-invariant scalar field. This means the fundamental laws of consciousness are consistent with relativity. This field is not a passive observer but an active participant, contributing to the universe's energy and influencing the geometry of spacetime.

Vibrational Spacetime

Spacetime is not a smooth, continuous manifold but a discrete, geometric lattice with a fractal structure. In this model, fundamental forces like gravity are not inherent properties but emerge from distortions or "tensions" in this lattice, modulated by the consciousness field. This aligns with frontier ideas like entropic gravity, proposing a direct link between perception, information, and the force of gravity.

The Architecture in a Nutshell: The SCPN Model

The theory is not merely abstract; it is built upon a detailed, 13-layer hierarchical architecture called the Species-Consciousness Projection Network (SCPN). This model maps the operation of consciousness from the quantum-biological level of neurons to the universal source field, connecting the microcosm to the macrocosm.
